E-Sports also known as electronic sports is a form of competition using video games.
Although organized online and offline competitions have long been a part of video game culture, the professional E-sports
scene started around the early 2000s.
By the 2010s, esports was a significant factor in the video game industry,
with many game developers actively designing toward a professional esports subculture.
The most common video game genres associated with esports are multiplayer online battle arena (MOBA) and first-person shooter (FPS).
Popular esports games include League of Legends, Dota 2, Counter-Strike, Call of Duty, Rainbow Six Siege, Overwatch
Those E-Sports streams are broadcasted on streaming sites like Twitch.
The biggest E-sports rech around 1 million viewers on Twitch alone.
Nowadays the prize pool of E-Sports tournaments reach from several hundred thousands of dollars to several millions of dollars.
We decided to mainly focus on the games League of Legends and Counter Strike.
On our website, we want to inform people about the current tornaments and the latest news around League of Legends and Counter Strike.
