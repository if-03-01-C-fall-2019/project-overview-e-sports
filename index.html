E-Sports also known as electronic sports is a form of competition using video games. Although organized online and offline competitions have long been a part of video game culture, the professional E-sports scene started around the early 2000s. By the 2010s, esports was a significant factor in the video game industry, with many game developers actively designing toward a professional esports subculture.
