E-Sports

Motivation

E-Sports also known as electronic sports is a form of competition using video games.
Although organized online and offline competitions have long been a part of video game culture, the professional E-sports scene started around the early 2000s.
By the 2010s, e-sports was a significant factor in the video game industry, with many game developers actively designing toward a professional esports subculture.
We decided to base out website around E-Sports because we bot love video games. Especially competitive games like League of Legends and Counter Strike are our favourites.

What are the most Important E-Sport Games?

The most common video game genres associated with esports are multiplayer online battle arena (MOBA) and first-person shooter (FPS).
The most popular esports games include :
FPS games like Counter-Strike, Call of Duty, Rainbow Six Siege, Overwatch & MOBAs like DOTA 2 and League of Legends.
On our website we decided to mainly focus on the games League of Legends and Counter Strike, because we both play those 2 games.

Where can I watch E-Sports?

E-Sports streams are broadcasted on streaming sites like Twitch.
Twitch is a website where people can stream how they play video games and other people can watch those streams.
Over the las couple of years video game streams have gained a lot of popularity.
The viewers like those streams not only because of the video games but also beacause the viewers can interact with thei favorite streamers.
The biggest E-sports can reach around over a million viewers around the world on Twitch alone.
