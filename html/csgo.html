The game Counter-Strike: Global Offensive (abbreviated CS:GO) was developed and released by Valve in 2012. Valve Corporation as an american video game developer and publisher based in Wasshington.

What is the game about?

Counter-Strike: Global Offensive is a First-person shooter. The games consists of several different game modes. But the the main game mode is the Competitive Mode. In Competitive Mode two teams consisting of 5 players compete against each other.The teams are called Terrorists and Counter-Terrorists (which are abbreviated as Ts and CTs). Both teams have the same weapons except for some exceptions. For example the Ts can buy a AK-47 which the CTs can't but the CT counterpart is the M4A4/M4A1S.
